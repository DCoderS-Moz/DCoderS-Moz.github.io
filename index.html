ABOUT US

D'CoderS was one of the first coding club of Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology under CSE Dept. It was founded in the year 2016 and has been actively organizing technical workshops and competitions ever since.

OUR AIM

D'CoderS aims to promote an active and dynamic coding culture in the campus and introduce students to various tools that will help them solve problems through their computers.

WORKSHOPS

Web Development

Learn to create websites like your personal online porfolio and boost your online presence.

Mobile Development

Learn to make amazing mobile apps using Android Studio and express your creativity.

Python

Learn Python which is one of the easiest programming language which makes implementing solutions easier.

MENTOR
